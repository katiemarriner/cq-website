About

Capitol Quest is a multiplayer mobile game that allows users to assume the role of local politicians and gain power through strategic play against other real users. Players pass bills, make backroom deals, gain political capital points and advance to the top. Capitol Quest is based on current events in the North Carolina General Assembly.
